Company Profile

Established in 2001, Chibros Concepts & Construction is an award winning, full service architectural and interior design firm based in Nnewi,Anambra,Nigeria. Our staff provides a broad range of professional services, with exceptional performance and extraordinary attention to detail. With a passion for excellence, expressed through design,Chibros Concepts & Construction prides itself on helping clients envision projects that meet their needs and then turn that vision into reality.

Whether it involves designing an office building from the ground up, retrofitting a shopping center to meet changing market needs or breathing new life into an aging housing complex, we have the skill and experience to deliver excellent, cost-effective results.

Chibros Concepts & Construction capabilities include virtually all site planning, exterior and interior design, and construction administration services. We specialize in retail, office, multi-family and commercial centers. We also specializes in revitalization projects. We have been instrumental in transforming numerous empty or underutilized shopping centers, highly functional spaces that can meet the changing demands of the retail marketplace.
